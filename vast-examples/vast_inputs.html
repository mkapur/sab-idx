VAST‌ ‌input‌ ‌descriptions‌ ‌for‌ ‌Ecologists‌ ‌ ‌
July‌ ‌2019‌ ‌
Contributors‌:‌ ‌Cecilia‌ ‌O’Leary,‌ ‌Dave‌ ‌McGowan‌ ‌
 ‌
 ‌
For‌ ‌VAST‌ ‌version‌ ‌‌VAST_v8_0_0‌ ‌
 ‌
To‌ ‌get‌ ‌information‌ ‌on‌ ‌data‌ ‌inputs‌:‌‌ ‌‌?VAST::make_data‌ ‌
To‌ ‌define‌ ‌VAST‌ ‌version‌:‌‌ ‌Version‌ ‌=‌ ‌‌"VAST_v7_0_0"‌‌ ‌‌or‌ ‌‌Version‌ ‌=‌ ‌get_latest_version(package‌ ‌=‌ ‌‌"VAST"‌)‌ ‌
Subscript‌ ‌definitions‌ ‌
Cell‌:‌ ‌g,‌ ‌‌Time‌:‌ ‌t,‌ ‌‌Covariate‌:‌ ‌p,‌ ‌‌Linear‌ ‌predictor:‌‌ ‌z,‌ ‌‌Category‌:‌ ‌c‌ ‌
Table‌ ‌of‌ ‌Contents‌ ‌
A.‌ ‌‌Spatial‌ ‌settings‌‌2‌ ‌
B.‌ ‌‌Model‌ ‌settings‌3‌ ‌
C.‌ ‌‌Specified‌ ‌outputs‌*‌5‌ ‌

E.‌ ‌‌Data‌ ‌&‌ ‌Covariates‌6‌ ‌

 ‌
*‌ ‌for‌ ‌information‌ ‌on‌ ‌VAST‌ ‌outputs,‌ ‌see‌ ‌‌this‌ ‌google‌ ‌doc‌ ‌
 ‌
A.Spatial‌ ‌settings‌ ‌
Define‌ ‌the‌ ‌spatial‌ ‌resolution‌ ‌of‌ ‌the‌ ‌model:‌ ‌‌Method,‌ ‌grid_size_km,‌ ‌n_x,‌ ‌Kmeans_Config‌ ‌
This‌ ‌is‌ ‌input‌ ‌into‌‌ ‌make_spatial_info()‌ ‌‌in‌ ‌addition‌ ‌to‌ ‌data‌ ‌sample‌ ‌Latitude,‌ ‌sample‌ ‌Longitude,‌ ‌and‌ ‌k-means‌ ‌Lat‌ ‌and‌ ‌Long.‌ ‌
Spatial_List‌ ‌‌=‌ ‌make_spatial_info(‌ ‌grid_size_km=grid_size_km,‌ ‌ ‌
‌n_x=n_x,‌ ‌ ‌
‌fine_scale=fine_scale,‌ ‌ ‌
‌Method=Method,‌ ‌ ‌
‌Lon=Data_Geostat[,‌'Lon'‌],‌ ‌ ‌
‌Lat=Data_Geostat[,‌'Lat'‌],‌ ‌ ‌
 ‌
LON_intensity=Extrapolation_List$Data_Extrap[which(Extrapolation_List$Data_Extrap[,‌'Include'‌]==‌TRUE‌),‌'Lon'‌],‌ ‌ ‌
 ‌
LAT_intensity=Extrapolation_List$Data_Extrap[which(Extrapolation_List$Data_Extrap[,‌'Include'‌]==‌TRUE‌),‌'Lat'‌],‌ ‌
‌Extrapolation_List=Extrapolation_List,‌ ‌DirPath=RunDir,‌ ‌Save_Results=‌TRUE‌‌ ‌)‌ ‌
 ‌
 ‌
Input‌ ‌
Definition‌ ‌
Example‌ ‌
Notes‌ ‌
Method‌ ‌ ‌
defines‌ ‌the‌ ‌mesh‌ ‌type‌ ‌
that‌ ‌is‌ ‌used‌ ‌
Method‌ ‌=‌ ‌c(‌"Grid"‌,‌ ‌‌"Mesh"‌,‌ ‌
"Spherical_mesh"‌)[‌2‌]‌ ‌ ‌
 ‌
Mesh‌ ‌is‌ ‌the‌ ‌default‌ ‌recommendation‌ ‌
grid_size_km‌ ‌ ‌
sets‌ ‌the‌ ‌size‌ ‌of‌ ‌the‌ ‌
survey‌ ‌
grid_size_km‌ ‌=‌ ‌‌25‌ ‌
this‌ ‌only‌ ‌matters‌ ‌if‌ ‌you‌ ‌are‌ ‌using‌ ‌
Method‌ ‌=‌ ‌‌"Grid"‌ ‌
n_x‌ ‌
Number‌ ‌of‌ ‌knots‌ ‌
n_x‌ ‌=‌ ‌c(‌100‌,‌ ‌‌250‌,‌ ‌‌500‌,‌ ‌‌1000‌,‌ ‌‌2000‌)[‌1‌]‌ ‌
 ‌
Kmeans_Config‌ ‌
controls‌ ‌the‌ ‌k-means‌ ‌
algorithm‌ ‌to‌ ‌define‌ ‌the‌ ‌
location‌ ‌of‌ ‌knots‌ ‌
Kmeans_Config‌ ‌=‌ ‌list(‌ ‌‌"randomseed"‌=‌1‌,‌ ‌
"nstart"‌=‌100‌,‌ ‌‌"iter.max"‌=‌1e3‌‌ ‌)‌ ‌ ‌
Use‌ ‌for‌ ‌‌Method‌ ‌=‌ ‌‌"Mesh"‌ ‌
